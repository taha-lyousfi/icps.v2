Pr. Yahya Tayalati

Professor of Physics, Mohammed V University in Rabat. Pr. Tayalati was part of the ATLAS collaboration at the LHC which led to the observation of the Light-by-Light scattering. He is an honorary fellow of the Islamic World Academy of Sciences and has been awarded the prestigious "Mustafa Prize" for all his contributions in physics.

Speaker bio

Rajaâ Cherkaoui El Moursli

Resident Member, Hassan II Academy of Science and Technology. High Energy Nuclear Physics - Faculty of Sciences, Mohammed V University in Rabat. She received the prestigious "L'Oréal-UNESCO Award for Women in Science". She made significant contributions to the ATLAS collaboration at the LHC/CERN, which led to the discovery of the Higgs Boson.

Speaker bio

Pr. Abdelilah Benyoussef

Resident Member, Hassan II Academy of Science and Technology. Pr. Benyoussef's main research interest is in Ab-initio calculation and Monte carlo method in Magnetism and Phase Transition, Energy (nanomaterials for photovoltaic, hydrogen storage and production, thermoelectricity), and Quantum Information.
