Call for Abstracts

Share your work with the ICPS community. Submit your abstract for a talk or poster at ICPS 2026 in Rabat, Morocco. Deadline: 7 July 2026, 23:59 (UTC).

Submit your abstract

Overview

ICPS 2026 welcomes abstract submissions from physics students and early-career researchers working on any area of physics, from fundamental research to applications in industry. Accepted abstracts will be presented as oral talks or posters during the conference, giving you the chance to share your work and get feedback from peers from around the world.

Whether you are presenting early-stage research, a completed project, or a review of your field, we encourage you to submit — ICPS is about learning from and connecting with fellow physics students, not just showcasing finished results.

Topics

We welcome abstracts across all areas of physics, including but not limited to:

Particle & High Energy Physics

Collider physics, particle detectors, and fundamental interactions.

Astrophysics & Cosmology

Stellar physics, gravitational waves, and the early universe.

Condensed Matter & Materials

Quantum materials, nanotechnology, and material characterisation.

Quantum Physics & Technologies

Quantum computing, quantum information, and quantum optics.

Biophysics & Medical Physics

Physics applied to living systems, imaging, and healthcare.

Physics in Industry & Data Science

Careers beyond academia, applied data science, and AI for physics.

Physics Education & Outreach

Teaching methods, science communication, and public engagement.

Other Areas of Physics

Any other physics topic not listed above — if in doubt, submit it anyway.

How to Submit

  1. Prepare your abstract (title, authors, affiliations, and a summary of your work).
  2. Fill in the submission form below before 7 July 2026, 23:59 (UTC).
  3. Watch your inbox — the Local Organising Committee will follow up with confirmation and next steps.
